About Davis Dheem Tana

Dheem Tana is the University of California Davis Annual Indian Classical Dance Competition. Our goal is to provide a platform for Indian Classical Dance teams to showcase their talent and passion for dance in a positive atmosphere, where dancers can meet and form bonds with others who share the same goals and interests. Davis Dheem Tana is hosted in partnership with Visions at UC Davis, a charity organization focused on the education of underprivileged children in India, Sri Lanka, and Ethiopia. Davis warmly welcomes you to its third annual Indian classical dance event, Davis Dheem Tana 2017!

About Visions Charity

Visions Global Empowerment is a LA-based global non-profit organization dedicated to youth education and empowerment in South India, Sri Lanka, and Ethiopia. Visions' mission is to change patterns of inequality by supporting educational initiatives for youth affected by poverty and conflict. Since 2003, Visions has collaborated with more than 30 local partners to develop a range of programs that create educational opportunities and respond to community needs. Visions endeavors to enable students, teachers, schools, and non-governmental organizations to become self-sufficient and capable of participating as leaders in today's society.
